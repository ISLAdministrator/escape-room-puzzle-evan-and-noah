Escape Library

“Now locked inside the library you begin to look for a way to escape when you notice books have been strewn across the floor from each section, no longer in order. Well I have nothing better to do, you think to yourself. You start to pick up the books from the floor when you realise the books are from all different sections of the library. When you step back you notice that you can see all of the sections clearly in a row. Use the order of the missing books as well as the numbers on the spine to unlock the computer screen”
